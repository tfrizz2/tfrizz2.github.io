Who We Are

Company Overview

Overages Claims Services US is focused on helping eligible property owners recover unclaimed tax overage funds through a clear and professional process.

We communicate directly, explain each stage of the claim, and provide practical support for documentation and submission requirements.

Our Mission

To empower property owners and rightful claimants by identifying, recovering, and returning tax overage funds that are rightfully theirs — delivering expert guidance, transparent processes, and compassionate service every step of the way.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and recognized leader in tax overage recovery, building a future where no rightful claimant loses their money to bureaucratic complexity — ensuring every dollar owed finds its way back home.
