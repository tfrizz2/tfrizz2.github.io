Overview

What We Do

Overages Claims Services US assists property owners in reclaiming unclaimed funds that can remain after tax sales or related proceedings.

Our role is to verify eligibility, prepare claim documentation, and guide clients through each step until release.

Nationwide Coverage

We support claimants across the United States and provide direct outreach when public records indicate possible unclaimed funds.

Questions about your notice? Use our contact form for a consultation and next steps.

Your Privacy Matters

We keep your information 100% confidential and will never request any personal financial details or payment information.

Trust & Compliance

Private Claims Service

Overages Claims Services US is a private service and is not a government agency. Property owners may file directly with the applicable government office if preferred.
