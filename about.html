About the Process

How Tax Overage Recovery Works

A tax overage can occur when a property sells for more than the amount owed to satisfy taxes, fees, and related costs. In some cases, the remaining amount can be claimed by an eligible party.

Overages Claims Services US explains this process, prepares documentation, and helps claimants navigate deadlines and filing requirements.

Claim Steps

  1. 1Identification of potential funds.
  2. 2Verification of claimant eligibility.
  3. 3Claim preparation.
  4. 4Submission to authorities.
  5. 5Government review and release.

What You May Need

Documentation requirements vary by jurisdiction, but commonly include identity verification, proof of property interest, and signed claim forms.

We help organize required records so your submission is complete and easier for the reviewing agency to process.

We are a private service and do not guarantee outcomes or timelines, which are controlled by the reviewing authority.
